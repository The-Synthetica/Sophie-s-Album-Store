Experimental Hyperpop albums

Sophie was an experimental pop producer. Hyperpop pioneer and an trans activist pop star. She worked with Madonna, Charli XCX, Lady Gaga and more...

She passed away, but still with us in her music. Her work and legacy are the foundation of the self expression and transactivism of every immaterial person. Thats the reason because "PRODUCT" and "Oil of every pearl's Un-insides are so estimated.
